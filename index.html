A brief description of myself

My whole life, I've been intrigued by how a computer can convert a simple electrical signal into thousands of different forms of content and entertainment. This led me to try to understand them, and I began my formation as an Electronic Engineer. Along the way, I've become interested in design and how, through technologies powered by electronics, we can create things that not only fulfill a function but also make human beings feel good: photography, graphic design, video editing, illustrations, cinema. All the things that intrigue and fascinate me can be reflected in web development. That's why I dedicate myself to learning about it every day.
