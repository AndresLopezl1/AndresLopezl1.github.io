Project 1

CanSat HIVE

A satellite designed to collect atmospheric information and transmit it wirelessly to a ground station. The basis of the prototype was perfboards, an Arduino, and a housing designed with 3D printing. My work was based on the prototyping and presentation of different operational alternatives using the Autodesk Fusion 360 program and the design and creation of the prototype's body.

Project 2

A Mini Game in STM32

A mini-game created using an STM32 microprocessor. As part of the team, I helped build the logic that operated the model. The game had a 'Space Invaders' style and was based on 3 levels with increasing difficulty. All the control and graphics on the 8x8 pixel screen were developed using the microprocessor's logic.
